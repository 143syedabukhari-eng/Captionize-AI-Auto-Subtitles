Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2024

Welcome to Captionize: AI Auto Subtitles ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles your information.

Transparency Disclosure: Captionize operates on a "Bring Your Own Key" (BYOK) model. Users are responsible for providing their own API keys for third-party services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data: When you sign in using Google Sign-In, we collect your basic profile information (name, email, and profile picture) via Firebase Authentication to manage your account and save your projects.

Media Content: To provide subtitle services, the app processes video and audio files selected by you from your device. These are uploaded to your personal Cloudinary account.

API Credentials: Any API keys you provide (AssemblyAI, Google Gemini, Cloudinary) are stored only locally on your device using hardware-backed encryption (EncryptedSharedPreferences). We never see or store these keys on our servers.

2. How Your Data is Processed

To generate subtitles, the following workflow is used:

  • Cloudinary: Your video is uploaded to your own Cloudinary storage.
  • AssemblyAI: The audio URL is sent to AssemblyAI for speech-to-text transcription.
  • Google Gemini: Transcripts may be refined using Google Gemini for grammatical accuracy.
  • Firestore: Subtitle text and project metadata are saved to your account in Google Firebase Firestore.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • All data transmissions are encrypted via HTTPS.
  • User API keys are encrypted at rest on the device.
  • Authentication is handled securely via Google Play Services and Firebase.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with several third-party providers. We recommend reviewing their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services / Gemini
  • Firebase
  • AssemblyAI
  • Cloudinary

5. User Rights & Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can delete your projects directly within the app at any time. If you wish to delete your entire account and associated data from our Firebase database, please contact us at the email below.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
